# Team Contributions - MedicTranslate

## Project Overview

**MedicTranslate** is an accessible web application developed for the [Buildathon by Enyata](https://buildathon.enyata.com/) that helps users understand their lab test results through image upload and AI-powered analysis. The application translates complex medical terminology into plain language, providing health education and guidance while emphasizing that it is not a replacement for professional medical care.
